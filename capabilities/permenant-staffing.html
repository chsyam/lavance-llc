Permanent Staffing

Hiring and integrating employees into the work culture are key corporate practices.

A business thrives on skilled and committed employees, making the right recruitment essential for success.

Lavance LLC, a trusted permanent staffing agency with 7+ years of experience, provides top-tier staffing solutions for businesses of all sizes. From startups to established enterprises, we help enhance work culture and drive growth.

Find the Right Employees

A strong employer-employee relationship drives organizational success. While contract staff can be useful, a core team of permanent employees is essential.

At Lavance LLC helps businesses find the right talent through our expert permanent staffing services. With a rigorous screening process, we ensure you hire dedicated and skilled professionals.



How Does it Work?

How Does Lavance LLC's Permanent Staffing Solution Work? We follow a structured and strategic approach to ensure top-quality recruitment. Candidates are carefully evaluated based on their skills, leadership qualities, and communication abilities. Here's how our process works:

  • 1Understanding the Requirements

    When hiring permanent employees, every company has unique needs. At Empakt Software, we carefully analyze your requirements to provide the best-fit candidates. Our process begins with in-depth discussions to understand your expectations and ensure a seamless recruitment experience.

  • 2Creating the Recruitment Plan

    We craft a custom recruitment plan tailored to your specific needs. This plan outlines the strategies we'll use to find top talent, ensuring efficiency and alignment with your expectations. A well-structured approach helps streamline the hiring process and reduces the time to recruit.

  • 3Screening Tests for Candidates

    Lavance LLC follows a multi-layered screening process to evaluate candidates based on skills and job relevance. Only shortlisted candidates move forward for interviews and document verification, ensuring a precise and efficient hiring process.

  • 4Interview and Document Verification

    We conduct face-to-face interviews to assess candidates' competencies for the job. Additionally, document verification ensures legal compliance, making the recruitment process seamless and reliable.

  • 5Job Letter and Salary Negotiation

    The final stage involves salary negotiation, ensuring fair compensation for employees while aligning with the client's budget. The process concludes with issuing detailed job offers to the selected candidates.
