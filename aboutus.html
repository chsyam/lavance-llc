About Us

The Empakt Difference

At Lavance LLC, we bridge the gap between talent and opportunity. Our mission is to recognize the skills of proficient IT professionals and connect them with the best recruitment solutions. With our seamless IT staffing services, we have successfully catered to businesses of all sizes—from startups to large enterprises. We understand that every company's hiring needs are driven by its unique goals, and we are committed to providing staffing solutions that contribute to your success.

Mission

Lavance LLC is committed to delivering high-quality IT staffing services that empower organizations. Since 2002, we have been dedicated to providing seamless and efficient staffing solutions, ensuring businesses find the right talent. Our mission is to tackle even the most challenging IT staffing assignments, catering to companies of all sizes—from startups to large enterprises—while maintaining excellence and reliability.

Vision

Our company was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between employers and job seekers in the global IT community. By connecting top talent with the right opportunities, we create value for both businesses and professionals, ensuring mutual growth and success.

Ethical IT Staffing Service

We deliver ethical staffing solutions that businesses can rely on while ensuring job seekers trust us to recognize and value their talents. At Lavance LLC LLC, our multi-layered screening process guarantees that enterprises hire only top-quality IT professionals.

The right recruitment not only drives financial success but also fosters a positive and competitive work culture within an organization.

Our Work Culture

At Lavance LLC, we follow the TOFFEE principle, which is built on six core pillars of business ethics:

T

Trustworthy: Reliable and Consistent

Our commitments are sacred to us, and we strive to deliver on time, every time.

A

Accountability: Responsible and Committed

We take full responsibility for our words, actions, and deliverables.

I

Integrity: Strong Morals and Ethics

Our business is driven by deep-rooted principles and unwavering moral values.

R

Respect: Humble, sensitive and empathetic

We treat everyone with respect and believe that empathy is the foundation of strong relationships.

R

Result Oriented: Strong processes, reliable outcome

With years of experience, we have built processes that ensure dependable and high-quality results.
